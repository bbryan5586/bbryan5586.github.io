About Our Store

Welcome to The Record Stop

Founded in 2010, The Record Stop has been the prime location to get your hands on vintage vinyls and new releases alike. We believe in the power of expression and emotion through the art of music.

Our collection is carefully curated by our team of dedicated music lovers, aiming to have as diverse of a stock as possible. From classic rock and roll to modern drum and bass, we have it all.

We are extremely passionate about connecting people with the music they love and identify with. Music, as an art, is powerful. We might just have the record for you. Give us a call, or come on over and look through our crates. We're always here to help and answer any questions you might have! See you soon!
