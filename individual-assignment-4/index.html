What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. In the modern era of the internet, cybersecurity is more important than it's ever been. Threats and vulnerabilities have been around since it's inception, but they have been evolving rapidly every single day. As new technology gets developed, so do the capabilities of hackers.

This site exists as a simple introduction to why cybersecurity is so important, the types of threats/vulnerabilities that you'll see abused out in the wild, and important resources that you should consider reading in order to identify threats before they target you.
